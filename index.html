Welcome to Non-Profit Helper

Non-Profit helper is a web application designed to assist a wide variety of non-profit organizations with data entry, search and find functionality. NPH is designed with your employee's productivity and ease of access in mind. Simply log in to the application or create a profile using any social media account. Within the app you can begin importing spreadsheets or entering in indviduals and their information. The app can then be used to search for available facilities for your clients.

Features

  • Data Mapping
  • Data Storage
  • Search Functionality
  • Trends & Favoriting
